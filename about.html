About Us

Unravelling The Cube is an online cubing competition that can be entered from anywhere. The competition is easy to enter and you can compete in just minutes for no cost! This competition is not officially sanctioned by the World Cube Association and is not affiliated with the WCA or endorsed by them in any way shape or form. This competition was initially organized by MMJ but now UTC is run by a group of volunteers.
